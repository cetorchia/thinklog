About Thinklog

Thinklog is intended to be a tool for helping you analyze your thoughts and the thoughts of others. Every day many of us enter a lot of data into social networks such as Twitter and Facebook, such as what we do, where we work, and who we know. However, is this data helping us? Why not put this data to work, for you?

The aim of Thinklog is to provide you with insights about thoughts that you enter into Thinklog, and thoughts that are out there on the web. You can enter your thoughts as they arise in your mind, or you can tell Thinklog to fetch RSS feeds or Twitter content. Thinklog processes this content so it can provide you with relevant keywords and recommended thoughts. The goal is to show how your thoughts relate to the thoughts of others and vice-versa, and to show trends in your personal experience.

Rather than being locked away in "social networking silos", useful insights into our own data can be open to the world, creating opportunity for growth for a wide range of businesses, communities, and individuals.
